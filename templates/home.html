About Us


Welcome to Young Linguists, where we offer a unique and engaging learning experience for teenagers taught by fellow teenagers. We understand that learning a new language can be intimidating, which is why we strive to create a comfortable and friendly environment for our students. Our tutors are not only knowledgeable in their language of expertise, but also relatable and understanding of the struggles and challenges of learning as a teenager. Whether you're looking to improve your grades, prepare for a trip abroad, or simply expand your language skills, we're here to help you reach your goals in a fun and interactive way.
